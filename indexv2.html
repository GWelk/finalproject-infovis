Did you know?


Some vaccines are directly responsible for death.

The Vaccine Adverse Events Reporting System (VAERS) was created to keep track of problematic side effects reported to healthcare providers. Each year the VAERS database keeps tabs on complications and deaths resulting from immunizations administered in the United States.

In 2017, the largest number of deaths from a type of vaccine was 39. These deaths resulted from a combined vaccine, consisting of two or more of the following: Diptheria, Tetanus, Pertussis, Polio, Hib, Hepatitis.

Not Death - "Just injuries"


Many patients don't have reactions as severe as death. A total of 38,916 injuries were reported as a direct result of immunizations in 2017.

Sources


The data seen here was extracted from the Vaccine Adverse Events Reporting System from the US Department of Health and Human Services.

Please note, the data from VAERS specifically states: "...the inclusion of events in VAERS data does not imply causality."
